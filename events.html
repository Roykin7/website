Maker Club 2025

Join STEMCity Labs' exciting Maker Club 2025, designed to ignite curiosity and foster innovation in kids and young adults through hands-on STEM activities. With a mix of new projects and fan-favorite tracks, our program offers project-based learning that keeps young minds engaged and inspired all summer long.

Learning Tracks:

  • • App Development (Ages 9–18): Create mobile apps using MIT App Inventor
  • • Coding & Design Innovators (Ages 8–18): Learn Python with Edublocks and design with Figma
  • • Engineering Design Pro (Ages 8–18): Physical computing with Raspberry Pi & 3D modeling
  • • Young Engineering Explorers (Ages 5–7): Basic electronics and coding with PictoBlox

What You'll Learn:

  • • Develop practical skills in coding, app development, and 3D design
  • • Explore real-world applications of STEM concepts
  • • Build problem-solving and teamwork abilities
