Opportunities at STEMCity Labs

At STEMCity Labs, we're building an Africa where STEM innovation, civic engagement, and education empower communities and transform lives. Explore exciting opportunities to join our mission as a job seeker, partner organization, school, or volunteer. Get involved today and shape the future of STEM in Africa!

Job Opportunities
  • What We Offer: Competitive salaries, professional growth, and the chance to work on impactful projects in a dynamic, collaborative environment.
  • Focus Areas: STEM education delivery, startup incubation, civic tech product development, and rural community engagement.
  • Who Can Apply: Professionals with expertise in STEM, education, technology, or project management, especially those with experience in African contexts.
  • How to Apply: Browse open positions on our Careers Page and submit your application. Stay tuned for roles in Uganda and beyond!
Tenders & Partnerships
  • Tender Opportunities: We invite organizations to bid on projects like developing STEM curricula, building civic tech platforms, or supplying training equipment for our innovation hubs.
  • Partnership Scope: Work with us to co-create programs, sponsor initiatives, or provide resources for schools and startups in sectors like EdTech, Clean Energy, and Health Tech.
  • Who Can Apply: Corporates, NGOs, tech firms, and suppliers aligned with our mission to empower African youth through STEM.
  • How to Get Started: View active tenders on our Tenders Portal or contact our procurement team to discuss partnership opportunities.
Schools Joining Our Programs
  • Programs Offered:
    • STEM Workshops: Hands-on sessions in coding, robotics, and science for primary and secondary students.
    • Digital, Data, & AI Literacy Training: Equip students with skills to thrive in the digital age.
    • Teacher Professional Development: Training to integrate STEM into classrooms with modern pedagogy.
  • Benefits: Inspire creativity, boost academic performance, and prepare students for STEM careers while fostering civic engagement.
  • Who Can Join: Public and private schools, universities, and vocational institutions across Africa.
  • How to Get Started: Register Your School to schedule a program or request a mobile STEM lab visit. Let's spark innovation in your classrooms!
Volunteering Opportunities
  • Volunteer Roles:
    • STEM Mentors: Guide students and startups in coding, AI, or civic tech projects.
    • Workshop Facilitators: Lead hands-on STEM or literacy sessions in schools or communities.
    • Community Organizers: Support outreach events like STEM festivals or hackathons.
    • Content Creators: Develop curricula, social media content, or impact stories.
  • Why Volunteer: Gain hands-on experience, inspire the next generation, and connect with a vibrant STEM ecosystem.
  • Who Can Apply: STEM professionals, educators, students, or anyone passionate about youth empowerment and innovation.
  • How to Get Started: Sign Up as a Volunteer and join our next training or event. Opportunities available in-person and virtually!

Why STEMCity Labs?

  • Impactful Mission: Be part of a movement empowering African youth to solve real-world challenges through STEM.
  • Inclusive Ecosystem: Connect with educators, entrepreneurs, governments, and corporates to drive change.
  • Proven Impact: Our programs have reached thousands of students, startups, and communities, creating measurable outcomes.
  • Commitment to Equity: We prioritize underserved regions, women, and youth to ensure inclusive growth.
