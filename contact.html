Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved with STEMCity Labs?

There are many ways to get involved! You can volunteer, partner with us, donate, or simply spread the word about our mission. Visit our Get Involved page for more details.

Do you offer internship opportunities?

Yes! We regularly offer internship opportunities for students and recent graduates. Please send us your resume and a cover letter explaining your interest in STEM education.

How can schools partner with STEMCity Labs?

We work with schools to implement our STEM programs and provide teacher training. Contact us to discuss how we can support your educational goals.

Are your programs free for students?

Many of our programs are offered free of charge to students, thanks to the generous support of our partners and donors. Contact us to learn more about program availability.

About STEMCity Labs

STEMCity Labs is dedicated to transforming STEM education in Africa through innovative solutions that integrate civic engagement and technology.
