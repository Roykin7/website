Our Story. Our People. Our Purpose.

Empowering Africa's Next Generation of Innovators

We're not just an organization—we're a movement. A community of dreamers, builders, and changemakers united by one vision: transforming STEM education across Africa.

Our Story

Every great mission begins with a simple belief. Ours started with the conviction that every young African deserves access to quality STEM education.

The Spark

2020

The idea for STEMCity Labs was born from witnessing the vast potential of African youth and the gaps in STEM education infrastructure.

Foundation

2021

STEMCity Labs was founded with a mission to create vibrant educational ecosystems across Africa.

First Impact

2022

Launched our first community programs, reaching over 500 students and 50 educators in our inaugural year.

Innovation Hub

2025

Established partnerships and launched the Tujulize Uganda civic tech platform.

Future Vision:Cultivating Africa's Innovators

2025 & Beyond

To scale, reach and impact 10 million students and youth across Africa, solidifying our position as the leading STEM education and Civic Technology Hub by 2040 through unparalleled leadership in fostering groundbreaking innovations and empowering a generation adept at problem-solving for Africa's sustainable development.
